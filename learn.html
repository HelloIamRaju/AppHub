1.Using Equation to solve simple business problems.

Comparing Graphic Design Agencies Based on Prices

Agency Equation Price per Unit (x) Base Price (c)
Agency A Y1 = 15K + 500 15 RS 500 RS
Agency B Y2 = 20K + 300 20 RS 300 RS

To make a wise decision, plug in the quantity you need (K) into both equations. This will give you the total prices Y1 and Y2. Let's say you need 20 units:

Agency Total Price (Y)
Agency A Y1 = 15 * 20 + 500 = 800 RS
Agency B Y2 = 20 * 20 + 300 = 700 RS

In this case, Agency B is more affordable. By following this approach, you can determine which agency suits your needs better.

Further Exploration:

Now, let's dive deeper and explore specific quantities to see which agency offers better prices. We'll calculate for 1 unit, 30 units, and 200 units:

Quantity (K) Total Price - Agency A Total Price - Agency B Best Option
1 515 RS 320 RS Agency B
30 950 RS 900 RS Agency B
200 3500 RS 4300 RS Agency A

However, there might be a quantity where both agencies have the same cost. We can find this point by equating the equations:

Y1 = Y2
15K + 500 = 20K + 300
200 = 5K
K = 40

So, at a quantity of 40 units, both agencies have the same cost.

By understanding and utilizing this simple equation technique, businesses can effectively model their costs and make informed decisions for various products.
